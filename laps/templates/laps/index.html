Welcome to After the Race!

As a novice motorcycle racer I always ask myself, am I getting faster? This tool is helping me answer that question.

This minimum viable product currently serves as a read-only view of my personal race data. Please click around to see if this is something you'd find useful. Before I go further and attempt to support account creation and multiple users, I'd like to get an idea of what interest there might be.

If you dig this, if you'd like to use it, or if you have any comments at all, please reach out!
